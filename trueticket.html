True Ticket

    An event ticketing application for artists and venues to connect with and to sell tickets to their fans in a safer way.

Overview

True Tickets is a ticket selling platform that allows people to sell and buy tickets. Its users are artists, vendors, and fans. In this project we help True Tickets develop a new mobile application for ticket buyers to have a better purchasing experience.

Research

INTERVIEW & BEHAVIOR MAP

Understanding users.

At the discovery phase of this project, we conducted user interviews in order to have a better understanding of the problem. We started the interview by asking 2 closed-ended questions to understand their basic preferences, then asked more relative open-ended questions. Based on interviews' habits and preferences of buying tickets, we constructed a behavior map.

What problems have we obsered?

  • People care about the quality of service more than the price.
  • People feel that lack of information causes them to spend wasted time doing research.
  • People sometimes worry that they may not be able to attend the concert due to unexpected commitments.

PERSONA

Defining target users.

To have a more clear concept of the target customer, we set up a persona based on the interview and behavior map. With the main information of background, preferences and critical needs, we referred to Matt throughout the entire product development process.

JOURNEY MAP

Thinking from users' shoes and finding opportunities.

To understand the user journey, we tested users with prototypes and a few open-ended questions. By observing their feelings and learning from their feedback, we analyzed the important characteristics required in the application.

What opportunities have we found?

  • People sometimes need to spend extra time to learn how to buy a ticket on the system. Hence, the process of buying tickets should be as easy and clear as possible.
  • Since tickets are bought a few weeks prior to the event, people sometimes misplace their paper tickets. Thus, we can provide a new paperless function.

Prototype

SKETCH & WIREFRAME

Thinking from users' shoes and finding opportunities.

After understanding the key features that are needed in the application, each of our team members drew a sketch based on our understanding and thinking. With five versions of the sketch, we then started to express our own thoughts and discuss each page and function. After a lively discussion, we designed a version that combines all our ideas. And we turned it into our first wireframe.

What main features have we decided?

  • Simplified the process of purchasing tickets.
  • Besides the purchasing function, we also designed extra features to provide resale, e-ticket, and other relative information.

USABILITY TESTING

Improving the design for a better solution.

We asked target customers to take a usability test. We started the test from a pre-test questionnaire and we asked them to perform a few tasks using our design. During the test, they not only clicked the button but also shared what they were trying to find and what they expected to see after each action. Based on the test results, we redesigned our wireframe and test again.

What designs have we changed?

  • We removed the search page function in the navigation bar and replaced it with a community function for users to keep in touch with artists and other fans that are going to join the same concert. We used a search bar with a filter to replace the whole search page function.
  • We moved the history ticket function into the personal page, and only kept the future tickets in the main ticket funtion on the nevigation bar.

Design


Explore

Matt is a big fan of Charlie Puth. Based on his preferences, we recommend to him all of Charlie Puth's and relative singers' upcoming events. Matt can also use the filter to search other conferences based on date, time range, price, location, and type.


Booking

Matt can check all e-tickets he bought for future concerts on the ticket page. Matt can also resell or refund his ticket easily on this page. On the day of the concert, his e-ticket will be automatically displayed on his mobile phone as he approachs the concert gate thus eliminating any extra steps.


Ticket Manage

Matt can check all e-tickets for future concerts. He can also resale or refund his ticket easily on this page. And the app will show an auto notification on his phone once he is close to the concert location on the concert's day.


Community

Artists and fans can interact here. Matt can see Charlie Puth's latest update. Matt can also communicate with other Charlie Puth's fans and join the next concert together.


Profile

Matt can manage and see all his favorite artists and events in his profile. He can click the image to the artists' profile page or the detail page of the concert. Matt can also receive the system message to know more upcoming events for his favorite artists.
