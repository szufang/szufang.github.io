True Ticket

    Event Ticketing Application

    For artists and venues to connect with and to sell tickets to their fans in a safer way.

Based on a new technology - Blockchain, True Tickets provides people a safer and more open way to sell and buy tickets. Artists control the price of the tickets from start to finish, even when the tickets are selling on the secondary market. The goal of this platform is to reduce scalpers and provide buyers with tickets at a fair price and directly benefit the artists instead of the unwanted price the users end up paying the scalpers.

Our high level goals for True Ticket were to:

1. Make the booking process faster and easier to fans.
2. Give fans a way to refund or resell tickets.
3. Provide a social media channel for artists and fans to interact.

Process Work

Step 1: Interview & Behavior Map

We interviewed 10 people to better understand our target customers. To understand this we constructed a behavior map.

Step 2: Persona

Based on the behavior map, we created a Persona. The handsome Matt.

Step 3: Journey Map

We analyzed the whole process from Matt's perspective to better understand the important charecteristics required in an application.

Step 4: Wireframe

Based on the Persona and Journey Map we started designing the first draft of the application.

Explore

Matt is a big fans of Charlie Puth. Based on his preferences, we recommend him all the Charlie Puth's and relative singers' upcoming events.

Booking

Matt decides to attend a Charlie Puth concert and he is able to buy the ticket in 3 easy steps.

Ticket Manage

Once he finishes booking his ticket he gets a E-ticket for the same. Matt is thrilled to see the resale and refund option which he can choose incase an emergency situation arises.

Community

Matt gets excited when he sees Charlie Puth is connecting with the fans and giving out details about the event on the application.

Profile

Matt can see all his favorite artists and events in his profile.
