True Tickets is a ticket selling platform that allows people to sell and buy tickets. Its users are artists, vendors, and fans. In this project we are helping True tickets develop a new mobile application for ticket buyers to have a better purchasing experience.

Process Work


Step 1
Interview &
Behavior map

At the discovery phase of this project, we conducted user interviews in order to have a better understanding of the problem. We started the interview by asking 2 closed-ended questions to understand their basic preferences, then asked more relative open-ended questions. Based on interviews' habits and preferences of buying tickets, we constructed a behavior map.


Step 2
Persona

To have a more clear concept of the target customer, we set up a persona based on the interview and behavior map. With the main information of background, preferences and critical needs, We referred to Matt throughout the entire product development process.


Step 3
Journey Map

To understand the user journey, we tested users with prototypes and a few open-ended questions. By observing their feeling and knowing their feedback, we thought an analyzed the important characteristics required in the application.


Step 4
Sketches &
Wireframe

After understanding the key features that are needed in the application, each of our team members drew a sketch based on our understanding and thinking. With five versions of the sketch, we then started to express our own thinking and to discuss each page and function. After a lively discussion, we came out with a version that combines all our ideas. And we turned it into our first wireframe.

Step 5
Usability test

We found some target customers and let them have a usability test. We started the test from having a pre-test questionnaire and we asked them to perform a few tasks on our design. And during the performing, they not only clicked the button but also shared what they are trying to find and what they expect to see after each action. And Based on the test result, we redesigned our wireframe and test again.

Final Result

Explore

Matt is a big fans of Charlie Puth. Based on his preferences, we recommend him all the Charlie Puth's and relative singers' upcoming events.

Booking

Matt decides to attend a Charlie Puth concert and he is able to buy the ticket in 3 easy steps.

Ticket Manage

Once he finishes booking his ticket he gets a E-ticket for the same. Matt is thrilled to see the resale and refund option which he can choose incase an emergency situation arises.

Community

Matt gets excited when he sees Charlie Puth is connecting with the fans and giving out details about the event on the application.

Profile

Matt can see all his favorite artists and events in his profile.
