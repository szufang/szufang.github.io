iOrder

    Preorder the meal

    A win-win solution for customers and restorants on maximize profit.

EXCELENT AWARDS

in International ICT Innovative Services Competition, 2014.

People spend too much time on waiting for meals.

A survey shows that people spent spent over half an hour a day on waiting their food in the restaurant. Although this may seem as a trifle in the short time, it is quite time-wasting for our life because it happens everyday.

Solution: People can order and reserve at one time.

When restaurants receive the reservations, they can prepare the meals before coustomers come, thus customers do not waste time on waiting for the food. Moreover, restaurants can buy fresher foods because they know the ingredients they need for the next day. Apparently, restaurant can also decrease the cost by do not buy the useless integrients.

In this project, my main work is to design and build the Android App for custormers. Thus, this page will focus on the details of App.

Discover

The main acitivity is friendly and inviting. Nana can find favor restaurants within 2 steps.

Choose

iOrder introduces each restaurant and shows the menu. Let Nana choose her favorite one.

Customize

Foods that Nana ordered can be customeized by her own flavor.

Reserve

Nana is easily able to sign in and reserve the restaurant she wants to go.

Keep

Nana can check her reservation and historical order easily.

Process Work

To build up a system, the first task was to define the main feature function for each part. For iOrder, based on the user experience, we designed a simple flow and easy-used interface. We also wireframed and did mock-ups of the general look and feel of the App.
